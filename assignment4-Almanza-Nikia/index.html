Disneyland Halloween Event

Oogie Boogie Bash

Don your favorite costume for a frightfully fun night of Characters, tricks and treats for Guests of all ages.

The spell’s been cast! On select nights this September and October, Disney California Adventure Park will host a separately ticketed, Halloween spooktacular that includes after-hours park access to treat trails, photo opportunities, entertainment, attractions, décor and more.

So, whether you’re a poised princess, galactic defender or fearless hero, there’s something incredible for all. Choose from 25 exciting nights—as ticket availability is limited, be sure to get yours while supplies last!

  • Early Entry to Disney California Adventure Park Arrive ahead and explore the park before the party!
  • Character Sightings Seize the chance to see some of Disney’s most popular Characters decked out in their Halloween costumes.
  • Special Décor & More Enjoy unique decorations, lighting, music and effects sure to set a not-too-spooky, family-friendly tone.
  • Mickey’s Trick & Treat Watch Mickey and friends as they headline a live show filled with not-so-scary Halloween tales.
  • Frightfully Fun Parade Don’t miss this spirited masquerade cavalcade starring Mickey, Minnie and some of your fave Disney Villains!

Event Information

  • Oogie Boogie Bash – A Disney Halloween Party at Disney California Adventure Park
  • Select nights September 9 through October 31, 2021
  • Party 6:00 PM to 11:00 PM
  • Disney California Adventure Park mix-in begins at 3:00 PM
  • General Admission tickets available July 13, 2021—starting at $114 per person
